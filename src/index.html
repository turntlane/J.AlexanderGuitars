About Us

J. Alexander Guitars

J. Alexander Acoustic Guitars, established by proprietor and craftsman Jeff Alexander, stands as the quintessential boutique guitar store, where each instrument is meticulously handcrafted from the ground up. Our mission remains unchanged since our inception - to embody the enchantment of the world's most revered vintage guitars within contemporary instruments designed to fulfill the demands of today's professional musicians.

From my Phoenix, Arizona workshop, I launched J. Alexander Acoustic Guitars to fuse the warm, classic tones of vintage acoustics with the contemporary musician's demand for reliability. My mission was to craft distinctive, high-quality acoustic guitars that resonate with their own stories, enriching the musical journey of every player.

Jeff Alexander Founder

Acoustic Guitars & Lutherie

Handcrafted acoustic guitars represent the pinnacle of lutherie, an art form where meticulous craftsmanship meets the soul of music. Each instrument is a masterpiece, born from the hands of skilled artisans who select the finest woods and dedicate countless hours to perfecting every detail. This dedication to traditional techniques and personal touch ensures that every guitar sings with unparalleled clarity, warmth, and resonance. In the world of handcrafted acoustic guitars, every strum tells the story of its creation, offering musicians not just an instrument, but a partner in their musical expression

J. Alexander Guitars epitomizes the art of lutherie with handcrafted acoustic guitars that blend tradition with innovation. Each piece, carefully shaped by skilled artisans using select woods, resonates with exceptional clarity and warmth. These guitars are not just instruments but extensions of the musician's voice, offering a unique partnership in musical expression
