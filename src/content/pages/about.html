About Us

Mission Statement

Our aim is to be a team of committed colleagues, devoted to outstanding leadership and innovation within the fretted instrument and string manufacturing sectors, all while honoring our roots and customs as a family-owned business.

In my Phoenix, Arizona workshop, I founded J. Alexander Acoustic Guitars, blending the rich, timeless sound of vintage acoustics with the modern musician's need for dependability. I aimed to create unique, premium acoustic guitars, each with its own narrative, to enhance the musical experience of every guitarist.

Jeff Alexander Founder
